# Chapter 2: Awakening 


![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c3f365e7-acc8-4457-a6b1-3691c42aa60d)


The Lucas and Helford households were among the founding and most powerful families of the empire. Their strength and influence were built upon a long history of producing great knights, unmatched in both skill and valor. It was no exaggeration to say that the patriarchs of these families were some of the strongest men to have ever existed, their names spoken with respect across the empire.
